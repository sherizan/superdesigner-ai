# Gold UT – Usability Research Report
**Date:** 26 Aug 2025  
**Study Type:** Usability Test  
**Participants:** 5 Botim users  

---

## 1. Goal of Study
Evaluate the usability of the Gold Mini‑Program and gather insights on:
- Understanding of product purpose
- Ease of buying & selling gold
- Interpretation of portfolio & earnings
- Pain points across end‑to‑end flow
- User expectations & perceived usefulness

---

## 2. Methodology
- Usability testing
- 5 participants
- Target persona: All Botim users

---

## 3. Pre‑Test Insights
- Gold investment experience:
  - Offline: 4 users
  - Online: 1 user (Sarwa)
- Investment background:
  - Stocks: 4 users
- Platforms used: eToro, Sarwa, CFI
- Feature discovery channels:
  - Word of mouth (2)
  - Notifications (2)
  - Homepage banners (1)
  - Self exploration (1)
  - Onboarding (1)

---

## 4. Task Findings

### T1 – Initial Impression
**Findings**
- Entry points:
  - Home page: 2/5
  - Money page: 2/5
  - All services: 1/5
- All users confused by the 3rd line in onboarding card.

**Recommendation**
- Revise onboarding microcopy, clearly explain leasing.

---

### T2 – Buy Gold (10g)
- Success: 5/5
- Ease rating: 4.3/5

**User Quotes**
- “Confusing status: Success → Processing → Purchased”
- “I want to share the receipt.”
- “Percentages (4%, 12%, 16%) are inconsistent.”

**Recommendations**
- Revise status microcopy  
- Add receipt sharing  
- Fix % calculation inconsistencies  
- Replace + / – with Buy / Sell labels

---

### T3 – Sell Gold (5g)
- Success: 5/5
- Ease rating: 4.3/5

**User Quotes**
- “Why is buy price higher than sell price?”
- “Need Sell All / Max option.”
- “Is it Sharia compliant?”

**Recommendations**
- Add Max/All option  
- Show 24K beside rate  
- Clarify leasing, OGold & Sharia compliance

---

### T4 – Portfolio
**User Quotes**
- “Earnings this month – when is it paid?”
- “Add AED labels to chart.”
- “Where are profits reflected?”

**Recommendations**
- Improve chart labels  
- Clarify earnings vs current value  
- Add sell fee info to FAQs

---

### T5 – Wrap‑Up Feedback
- “Reward first transaction.”
- “Earnings not catchy for blue‑collar users.”
- “How much are sell fees?”
- “Is the 4% halal?”

---

## 5. Key Findings Summary
- All users completed buy & sell flows
- Avg ease of use: 4.3 / 5
- Major friction from unclear copy & missing trust information

---

## 6. Recommendations

### Content & Clarity
- Rewrite onboarding microcopy
- Explain leasing, OGold & Sharia compliance
- Add sell fee info to FAQs

### UI & Features
- Add receipt sharing
- Replace + / – with Buy / Sell
- Add Max/All on sell
- Show 24K beside rate
- Add AED labels on charts

### Engagement
- Highlight earnings clearly
- Offer first‑transaction reward
- Mention international certification
